About Me

Engineer. Not just a theorist.

I'm a Security Engineer based in Casablanca, Morocco, working at INEOS on enterprise infrastructure projects for multiple client organizations across Morocco. My work is production — not simulations, not labs.

A typical week means configuring FortiGate firewall policies, pushing config across dozens of sites through FortiManager, tuning SD-WAN path selection, debugging IPsec tunnel issues, deploying a Palo Alto in HA, or staging a new branch device for zero-touch rollout. The details matter when you're working on live infrastructure.

I also hold a practical background in penetration testing and offensive security — Red Team attack simulation, SIEM detection engineering, MITRE ATT&CK mapping. That perspective shapes how I design firewall policy and think about segmentation.

Engineering Degree in Cybersecurity from ISGA Marrakech. Actively working toward Fortinet NSE 4 and Cisco CCNA.
